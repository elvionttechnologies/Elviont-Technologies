AleaLens Support

Help for AleaLens and AleaLens Pro

AleaLens is a stock-research dashboard for Serenity research signals, live quote overlays, and company thesis summaries. It is a research tool only and does not execute trades or provide investment advice.

AleaLens Pro

AleaLens Pro is an Apple auto-renewable subscription priced at EUR 2.99 per month. Eligible users receive a 3-day free trial before monthly billing starts. Apple shows the final localized price, taxes, trial eligibility, and renewal terms before purchase.

Restore Or Manage A Subscription

  • Open AleaLens and use Restore Purchases on the paywall if you already subscribed.
  • Manage or cancel the subscription from Apple Subscriptions: https://apps.apple.com/account/subscriptions.
  • Billing, refunds, payment methods, and trial eligibility are handled by Apple.

Subscription Not Showing The Trial

If the 3-day trial does not appear in Apple's purchase sheet, confirm that the subscription's introductory offer is active in App Store Connect, available in the user's storefront, and that the Apple ID has not already used the trial for the same subscription group. Sandbox metadata can also take time to sync after a change.

Market Data

AleaLens uses public market-data sources for quote overlays. Market prices may be delayed or temporarily unavailable depending on provider availability and exchange rules.
