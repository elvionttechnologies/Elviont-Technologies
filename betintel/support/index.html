How can we help?

Get help with subscriptions, match data, AI answers, bet records, and app issues.

Contact Support

Contact the developer through Apple on the BetIntel App Store listing. Include a short description of the issue, your device model, iOS version, app version, and screenshots when helpful.

Quick Help

Subscription Access

If access does not unlock after purchase, reopen BetIntel and use Restore Purchases. You can manage or cancel subscriptions in Apple ID Settings.

Match Data

Fixtures, scores, and live match status come from public sports data sources. Live updates can occasionally be delayed by the provider.

AI Answers

BetIntel AI gives short match-focused answers using available match context. Responses are informational and are not betting advice.

Bet Records

Manual records are stored on your device. If you delete the app or clear app data, local records may be removed.

Before You Contact Us

  • Check that you are using the latest version of BetIntel.
  • Restart the app if match data looks stale.
  • For subscription issues, confirm the purchase appears under Apple ID Settings.
  • For a missing fixture or flag, include the competition name and teams.

Privacy And Legal

The BetIntel Privacy Policy explains local storage, match data, AI processing, and subscriptions.

BetIntel is an informational analytics tool. The app does not accept bets, deposits, withdrawals, or wagers.
